Grant Lefgren

Grant has spent his entire career in the home building industry. With over 15 years of management experience, Grant is qualified to manage both large and small residential projects while maximizing efficiencies and home owner satisfaction. Prior to co-founding Keystone Construction, Grant was VP of Operations for DR Horton in Utah with responsibility for purchasing, estimating, field operations, land development, customer service, architecture, and design studio. Although Grant has spent many years in senior management positions, he has also been “in the trenches” working as both a field superintendent and a sales agent, allowing him to have a unique perspective towards the small details and getting things done right.

Mike Horan

Over the past 20 years, Mike has successfully grown Companies from small local operations to large multi-million dollar regional companies with offices in Colorado, Utah, California and Hawaii. Mike focuses on establishing a win-win situation for all by promoting honesty, integrity, and hard work. While owner of Pine Valley Power, Mike grew his company in 2000 from a startup company operating out of his basement, to over $33 million in annual revenue. Although much of Mike’s experience has been in building transmission and distribution power lines and substations, he has demonstrated the skills necessary to grow a small company into a large and successful regional company respected within the industry.
